# Week 2 Cleaning Rules

## Cleaning Rules Used

- Remove extra spaces
- Remove duplicate whitespace
- Remove empty lines
- Merge broken line breaks
- Preserve paragraph structure

## Known Limitations

- Complex PDF layouts may still produce formatting issues
- Tables are not preserved correctly
- Multi-column PDFs may extract text in the wrong order

## Future Improvements

- Better paragraph detection
- Table extraction support
- Header and footer removal
- Improved bullet point preservation
